Account Deletion

Delete Your SoberSpace Account

SoberSpace lets users request account deletion from inside the app. This page explains how deletion works and what may be retained.

Effective date: May 29, 2026

How to Delete Your Account in the App

  1. Open SoberSpace.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete account.
  4. Confirm the deletion prompt.

If You Cannot Access the App

Email support@soberspace.app from the email address connected to your account. Include your username if you know it. We may need to verify your identity before deleting the account.

What Is Deleted or Deactivated

  • Your account access is disabled.
  • Your private profile details are removed or deactivated.
  • Your authentication credentials are invalidated or made unusable.
  • Some visible account information may be anonymized so community threads, safety records, and service integrity remain understandable.

What May Be Retained

We may retain limited information where reasonably necessary for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, safety investigations, dispute resolution, backup restoration, or enforcement of our Terms and Community Guidelines. Examples may include reports, blocks, moderation records, audit logs, transaction-like service records, and content needed to preserve the integrity of conversations or safety reviews.

How Long Deletion Takes

In-app deletion is designed to take effect promptly for account access. Backup copies and retained safety or legal records may take longer to expire according to normal retention schedules.

Deleting the App Is Not Account Deletion

Removing SoberSpace from your device does not delete your SoberSpace account. Use the in-app deletion flow or contact support.
