SoberSpace Support and Safety

Use this page for app support, account help, privacy requests, safety concerns, and store review support links.

Effective date: May 29, 2026

Not for emergencies. SoberSpace support cannot provide emergency help, medical care, therapy, detox services, crisis response, or urgent monitoring. If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call emergency services now.

Contact Support

Email support@soberspace.app for help with account access, app issues, privacy requests, safety concerns, moderation appeals, or questions about SoberSpace.

Report Content or Users

The fastest way to report safety issues is inside the app. Use the report menu on posts, comments, groups, chats, messages, or profiles. Reports may be reviewed along with related content and account information so we can enforce our Terms and Community Guidelines.

Block Users

You can block users in the app when you do not want them to interact with you. Blocking is a personal safety tool and can be used even if the other person has not violated a rule.

Account Deletion

You can request deletion in the app from Settings. For details, see the Account Deletion page.

Privacy Requests

For privacy or data requests, email support and include the email address or username associated with your account. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

Moderation Appeals

If you believe content removal, account suspension, or another moderation action was a mistake, email support with your username and relevant context. We may not share details about other users or confidential safety signals.
